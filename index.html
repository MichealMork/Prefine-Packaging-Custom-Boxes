Types of Prefine Packaging Custom Boxes

Prefine Packaging produces a wide range of custom box styles suitable for different industries and product categories:

Rigid boxes are commonly used for luxury items such as perfumes, watches, and gift sets. Corrugated boxes are ideal for shipping and heavy-duty storage. Folding cartons remain popular for retail, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and food packaging. Display boxes enhance product visibility at point-of-sale counters, while mailer boxes dominate modern e-commerce packaging.

Materials Used for Custom Box Manufacturing

Material selection defines the strength and sustainability of any packaging solution. Prefine Packaging uses cardboard for lightweight retail packaging, Kraft paper for eco-friendly branding, corrugated board for shipping protection, and rigid chipboard for premium presentation boxes.

These materials can be customized with additional layers for moisture resistance, impact protection, and luxury finishing.

Advanced Printing and Finishing Options

High-quality printing transforms ordinary packaging into a powerful branding asset. Prefine Packaging offers offset printing for crisp graphics, digital printing for short runs, and flexographic printing for bulk production.

Finishing options include matte and gloss lamination, foil stamping, embossing, debossing, UV coating, and window cutouts. These enhancements increase product attraction while maintaining packaging durability.

Custom Box Design Process Explained

The process begins with requirement analysis to determine box size, product weight, and branding goals. Next comes material selection, followed by artwork design and digital mockups.

A physical sample is approved before bulk production begins. After manufacturing, quality inspection ensures color accuracy, structural strength, and finishing consistency before shipment.
